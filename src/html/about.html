Dunkin’ Donuts Lebanon was the first to introduce donuts and filtered coffee to the Lebanese society. According to a survey conducted by PARC (Pan Arab Research Center), Dunkin’ Donuts Lebanon has proved to be the top coffee shop and hang out place for people of most age groups for 3 consecutive years. In addition DD visitors have registered the highest loyalty out of all the coffee shops in Lebanon with more than 50% of its customers visiting it most often. For more than a decade, Dunkin’ Donuts Lebanon has become the prime destination for coffee and donut lovers!

In Dunkin’ Donuts Lebanon stores we offer a wide variety of 35 donuts which customers can choose from as well as the new premium line for the more adventurous customers. Let us not forget our beverages that include hot and iced coffees, lattes and cappuccinos, hot chocolate, iced tea, Coolatta cold drinks and many more. In addition, Dunkin’ Donuts Lebanon serves delicious baked goods, croissant sandwiches, diet snacks, pre-packed sandwiches and salads.

It was in 1998 that Dunkin’ Donuts opened its first branch in Lebanon on Zalka highway. Over a decade later, the Lebanese community’s favorite coffee shop now has 30 branches all over the country! We have expanded in the greater Beirut area, with a branch in Hamra Main Street that was opened in the presence of the president and CEO of Dunkin’ Donuts worldwide. Our most recent addition includes a branch in St Nicolas, Ashrafieh.

Keep an eye out for new branches coming up!
